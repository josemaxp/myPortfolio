BirdPedy

BirdPedy is an app made with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose for birding in Spain. With the app, you can keep track of the birds you have discovered, see sightings made by other people, earn achievements for making those sightings and discoveries, and compare them with your friends.

Aquaria

Aquaria is a website created for aquarium setup from some parameters. These parameters, like liters, luminosity, pH, gH, etc., look for matches in all the species registered in a JSON to inform you which ones are suitable according to those parameters. It is still in process.

Python Bot

Twitter bot created to inform about the conservation state of living beings. The bot will update every day the changes of the conservation states. In addition, you can search for information about a specific living being. Due to Twitter's limitations, the bot has been disabled, but its tweets are still visible.
